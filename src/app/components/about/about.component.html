More about EOP..

EOP Healthcare Supplier aim at providing and supporting our client with best products and services in order to reduce the risks which come with managing and maintaining a medical practice. EOP healthcare Supplier offers REFURB, USED and New medical systems and enablers by using a SMART STRATEGY in order to curb expenses while practicing safe medicine. This is achieved through making sure we provide Affordable, reliable and trustworthy products which are environmentally friendly. In addition we offer best customer service with different flavors of service products.
